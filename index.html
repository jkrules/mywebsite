Who am I?

Joel Kandiah is a highly motivated, conscientious and skilled student. He currently in his final year of a Masters in Mathematics at Warwick University and working part time on the LIFE project with Nuffield Department of Medicine. He has a wide-ranging knowledge and experience with new technologies and science with a passion continued throughout curricular and extra-curricular activities. His interests have lead to work experience involving programming, maths, data science and medical statistics.

For his Masters he is working on a project to create a new epidemiological model potentially on the spread of COVID-19.

In future, he hopes to continue to work in the areas of Epidemiology and/or Data Science.
